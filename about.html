about.

Kendra Hettig is a graduate from California State University, Long Beach with a Bachelor of Arts in Communication Studies and a minor in Public Relations. Currently, she is looking for various opportunities within marketing or public relations where she can share her passion for content development and communications. She can be described using three adjectives:

Mission-Driven

As part of Kendra’s experience in marketing and public relations, she interned for the NAACP — a historical non-profit organization dedicated to social equality for all. During her internship, she learned the principles of creating captivating stories that promote a mission. Kendra worked alongside community leaders from diverse backgrounds and learned what it means to effectively spark change in a positive way.

Dedicated

Her communications background also includes a marketing internship with MemorialCare Long Beach Medical Center and Miller Children’s and Women’s Hospital Long Beach. As an intern for the two non-profit hospitals, she handled press and media inquiries, social media content development and campaign strategizing. Her work focused on educating patients, families and the community at large about various health topics and the resources MemorialCare offers.

Curious

Kendra’s other interests include bridging UX/UI design, creative copy and corporate social responsibility (CSR) with marketing/public relations. She enjoys learning new skills and staying inspired by the power of digital content and storytelling.
